Who We Are?

Glob TechPower Foundation (GTPF) is a non profit organisation driven by a shared vision. A vision that arose out of diverse backgrounds and experiences of professionals who came together with a common goal to use technology and digital innovation for positive and constructive educational, social as well as community impact. We believe in the potential of technology and it’s ability to transform lives and uplift communities from the underserve and marginalize background. This united commitment sparked the birth of Global Technopower Foundation (GTPF).
