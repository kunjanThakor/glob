Introduction

Together, Let's Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi!

We are excited to launch the "Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi" campaign, a transformative initiative to enrich orphanage students' lives through coding education with our ‘Coding on Wheels’ project. This noble effort empowers young minds with essential coding skills, fostering self-independence and a dignified life. "Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi," meaning "Learn and Teach," aims to educate these children so they can uplift their communities. We invite compassionate individuals, corporates, and well-to-do families to support this cause. Donations are eligible for 50% tax exemption under section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Together, we can brighten these young minds' futures.
