APPROACH

  • Accessible Education 🚌

    • Coding on Wheels brings a mobile learning unit to government schools, providing access to technology and quality education to students who may not have it otherwise.

  • Interactive Learning 🎓

    • The project uses a project-based learning approach, encouraging students to work on real-world projects and gain practical experience.

  • Mentorship Program👩‍💻

    • The mentorship program helps students to build relationships with industry professionals, gain insights into the tech industry, and develop the skills they need to succeed in their careers.

  • Collaboration with Schools🤝

    • The project works with schools to develop a curriculum that meets the needs of students and aligns with the school's goals
