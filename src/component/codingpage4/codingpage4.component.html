HOW IT WORKS

Partnership with Orphanages

GTPF collaborates with orphanages to identify suitable students and schedules regular visits by the mobile learning unit.

Assessment

Before starting the program, students undergo an initial assessment to gauge their prior knowledge and skill level.

Lessons and Workshops

The mobile learning unit visits the orphanages on a predetermined schedule, delivering interactive lessons and workshops.

Project Work

Students work on real-world projects during and between sessions, allowing them to apply their skills and gain practical experience.

Feedback and Progress Tracking

Instructors provide regular feedback and track students' progress throughout the program.
