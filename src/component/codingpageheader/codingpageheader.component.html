INTRODUCTION

The "Coding on Wheels" project by Global Technopower Foundation aims to bridge the digital divide for destitute students in orphanages. Equipped with cutting-edge technology and expert instructors, a mobile learning unit provides interactive coding lessons, workshops, and activities to introduce these students to the world of technology. The project is dedicated to empowering the most vulnerable members of society and giving them access to opportunities in the digital age.
