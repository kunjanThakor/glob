HOW IT WORKS

Partnership with Government Schools

We partner with government schools to identify students who would benefit from coding education and schedule regular visits by our mobile learning unit.

Initial Assessment

Before starting the program, students undergo an initial assessment to gauge their prior knowledge and skill level in coding and computer science.

Coding Lessons and Workshops

Our mobile learning unit visits the government schools on a predetermined schedule, delivering interactive coding lessons and workshops tailored to the students' needs.

Project-Based Learning

Students work on real-world coding projects during and between sessions, allowing them to apply their skills and gain practical experience.

Feedback and Progress Tracking

Our instructors provide regular feedback and track students' progress throughout the program, ensuring they stay on track and achieve their coding goals.
