"Shaping Futures: Join Us to Empower Orphan and Government School Students!"

Welcome to "Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi," a pioneering campaign that merges corporate growth with social responsibility. This unique initiative offers your company and employees transformative technology training while supporting a noble cause. By enhancing your team's skills, we aim to uplift orphanage students and underprivileged children in government schools through coding education, facilitated by our non-profit organization. Engage your employees in cutting-edge training, with a minimum donation of 5000 rupees per participant. Donations directly fund coding education for these children and are eligible for 50% tax exemption under section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Join us to make a difference.
