1) Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi

"Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi" is a transformative campaign dedicated to providing coding education to orphanage students through the initiative 'Coding on Wheels'. Recognizing the pivotal role education plays in empowering individuals, the campaign seeks financial support, encourages spreading awareness, and invites corporate partnerships. Donors benefit from tax exemptions under section 80G of the Income Tax Act. By joining forces, the campaign aims to create a future where every child, irrespective of their background, can dream and achieve.
