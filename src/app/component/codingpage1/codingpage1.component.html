Coding on Wheels

BENEFITS

> Empowering Vulnerable Students:

The project empowers destitute students in orphanages by providing them with access to coding education, which equips them with valuable skills for the future.

> Bridging the Digital Divide:

By bringing cutting-edge technology and expert instructors directly to orphanages, the project helps bridge the digital gap that often exists for marginalized communities.

> Nurturing Essential Skills:

In addition to coding skills, the project focuses on nurturing creativity, critical thinking, and a thirst for knowledge among students, preparing them for success in various aspects of life.

> Enabling Self-Reliance:

By providing students with the tools and resources they need to grow socially and economically, "Coding on Wheels" aims to create self-reliant individuals who can contribute positively to society.
